W209 Project: Change.org Twitter Analysis Tool

Visualizations Background Team


This dashboard was built for Change.org to conduct social media analytics on petition campaigns for fighting violence against women in India. The data represented here comes from a live petition, cast as "Justice for Kathua Rape Victim: 8yr old girl raped and murdered in Kathua, J&K". The petition can be found here.

Hover over the red question mark in the upper right-hand corner of each visualization to read an explanation and learn how to use it. Data source: Twitter filtered by search terms entered by the user (e.g. "Kathua","India","rape").

Overall Petition Status

These gauges show the overall petition status for this petition.

The first gauge measures the count of petition signatures on change.org.

The next three metrics show the overall engagement, unique twitter users, unique tweets, and number of followers that have seen the tweets.

Engagement Metrics

Tweets Over Time Tweets Heat Map
This is the tweets by time period for the hashtags.

You can use your mousewheel on the upper larger chart or use the lower smaller chart by grabbing the edges to zoom into a timeframe. Also when zoomed in you can move the greyed 'window' area in the lower chart around to view different time periods.
There is another chart in a separate tab also - click on the Tweets Heat Map to see the # of tweets based on day (Mon-Sun) and time.

Influencer Graph

This chart shows the users with the most connected followers for the hashtags.

Each node represents a twitter user with their followers arrayed around them.

Hover over the circles to see that name of that user. You can also drag the circles for a better view of the nodes.

Tweets By Location

This is the tweets by location for the hashtags

Each user's location is plotted on the chart. The location is defined by primarily the location data on the tweet or secondarily the location data from the account (that's why some are in odd places like Antarctica).
